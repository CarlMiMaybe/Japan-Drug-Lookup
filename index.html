General MHLW quantity rules

  • Prescription drugs: up to 1 month supply without Import Confirmation.
  • Other drugs and quasi-drugs: up to 2 months supply.
  • Injectable drugs and self-injection kits: up to 1 month supply.
  • External-use drugs: up to 24 items per product.

Controlled substances

  • Narcotics and stimulant raw materials need advance permission before travel.
  • Psychotropics follow separate amount thresholds and may need a doctor's certificate.
  • Some substances are prohibited even with a prescription.

What this app does

  • Maps common U.S. brand names to active ingredients.
  • Shows the likely Japan entry status for personal use.
  • Links the decision to official MHLW and NCD source pages.
